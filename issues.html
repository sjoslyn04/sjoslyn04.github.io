Issues

Housing

The housing affordability crisis has been a top priority for me throughout my tenure on the Council. Despite challenges posed by scarce resources on the Monterey Peninsula, we cannot shirk our responsibility. Housing is a fundamental right of all people, and we must do our part.

Clean Water

As the representative for the City of Monterey on Monterey One Water (M1W), our region’s wastewater and recycled water treatment agency, I spearheaded efforts to advance the expansion of Pure Water Monterey (PWM). This initiative utilizes advanced water recycling technology to deliver a new source of clean, safe, and sustainable water for Monterey.

Transparency

As Monterey’s Mayor, I am committed to enhancing citizen engagement and transparency in local governance. I will work closely with the Neighborhood and Community Improvement Program (NCIP) to empower our community. Additionally, I am committed to ensuring broader representation of community members on city boards, commissions, and committees.

Fiscal Responsibility

I am proud to have led efforts to significantly bolster our financial reserves. We increased the city’s rainy-day fund to 20% of the general fund, the largest reserve in our history. Additionally, I spearheaded the creation of 9 new reserve funds, including allocations for critical areas such as the Conference Center, Sports Center Facilities, Technology Infrastructure, Sea Level Rise, City Facilities, Library Facility and Maintenance, and Public Safety Facility and Maintenance.
